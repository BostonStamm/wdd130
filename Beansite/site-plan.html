Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been in the middle of a book series and come across something that you don’t entirely remember? Flipping back through every book to try and find what you’ve forgotten can be very tiring and frustrating. To solve this, I am working on a website that not only can include the information that you’re looking for in an easily searchable way, it will house other useful tools for anyone who is reading or aspiring to become a writer.

Audience

The audience that I will be directing my efforts toward is the consumers of my writing. The people who find it entertaining and wish to know more. It will also be a resource for those who wish to get into writing, themselves, as I will include resources and tips.

Content

Home page

The home page, the landing page, will serve to introduce the reader to a selection of choices as well as various other fun things. There will be the two main links to pages 1 and 2, the information service and the writing assistance tools and tips respectively. The other links will be to various sites that I find fun and want to share, such as this link to a cat photo service or to my social media and other personal affects.
